Diversity and Inclusion

The fact that the web development field is so inclusive and diverse is another reason I want to work in it. The field of IT as a whole has proven to be quite inclusive of LGBT and neurotypical individuals.

It's important to appreciate the opportunity to work in a field where you're respected. The possibility of working in such a supportive environment inspires and motivates me to reach my full potential as a web developer.
